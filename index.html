Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is a highly acclaimed and widely popular fantasy television series that originally aired on HBO from April 17, 2011, to May 19, 2019. Created by David Benioff and D. B. Weiss, the show is based on the "A Song of Ice and Fire" novels by George R.R. Martin.

What is Game of Thrones?

The plot

The show covers the battle between the lords of the continent of Westeros — most notably members of the Baratheon, Lannister, and Stark families — who are involved in the fight for the Iron Throne. This follows the rise of Daenerys Targaryen, the last member of an exiled family as she builds an army in Essos to reclaim the throne.

Who are the main characters in Game of Thrones?
